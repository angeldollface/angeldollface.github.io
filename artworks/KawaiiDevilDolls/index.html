Kawaii Devil Dolls

A satchel of demonic cuteness.

AMBER

Amber likes devilishly fast cars, pink shoes, and can’t get enough of reading Dr. Faust! She persists that she dated him for some time.

MANDY

Mandy is a feisty doll with a penchant for the demonic and a passion for all things cute and deranged.

MONIQUE

Monique would really like to be some sort of deranged doll from hell or the underworld but she simply doesn’t know how to go about it. Instead, she just dyes her hair purple. Because why the hell not?

MAYA

Maya is a bit weird. She claims to have been human in her former life but I think she might be a zombie. Her eccentric taste for human flesh confirms this.

ANGELIQUE

Angelique unlike her kin, likes the sun and bright things but will never open the curtains in her house. I tried to convince her to get some Vitamin D pills.

FLEUR

Fleur lives in a Barbie World. She likes pink and other nice girly colors but hates her visits to her lord and master, the devil.
